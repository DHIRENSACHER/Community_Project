Core Subjects

History: Study of past events, societies, and cultures — critical for research, teaching, and civil services.

Political Science: Understanding governance, political systems, public policies, and international relations.

Sociology: Study of society, social relationships, and social behavior — useful for social work, research, policy making.

Psychology: Understanding human behavior, mental processes, counseling, and therapy.

Literature: Exploration of languages, poetry, prose, and literary criticism — relevant for writing, media, and teaching.
