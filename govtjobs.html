Government Jobs After Schooling

Explore secure career options after 10th and 12th standard.

Government Jobs After 10th

SSC MTS: Multi-Tasking Staff recruitment under Staff Selection Commission for clerical/admin roles.

Railway Group D: Jobs in Indian Railways like trackman, helper, assistant pointsman, etc.

Defense: Tradesman and civilian posts in Army, Navy, Air Force.

State Boards: Peon, office assistant, clerk under various state government departments.

Government Jobs After 12th

SSC CHSL: Combined Higher Secondary Level Exam for LDC, Postal Assistant, Data Entry Operator.

Railway Clerk/Typist: Various Railway Recruitment Board posts like clerk, ticket collector, typist.

Defense: NDA for officer training (Army, Navy, Air Force). Other paramilitary forces like CISF, BSF, CRPF.

State PSC: Clerical exams, junior assistant posts in state public service commissions.
