PCM (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics)

Engineer

PCB (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)

Doctor

Pharmacist

Work in hospitals, drug stores, or pharmaceutical companies.

Biotechnologist

Genetics, agriculture biotech, medical research, life sciences.

Note: Students with PCMB (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Biology) are eligible for all the above career options.
